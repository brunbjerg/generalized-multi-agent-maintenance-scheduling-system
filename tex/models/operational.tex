The $\VarStartOfSegment{a}{k}$ is the start time of job $i$ in segment $k$ and $\VarFinishOfSegment{a}{k}$ is the finish time of job $i$ in segment $k$.
$\VarProcessingTime{a}{k}$ is the processing time of each segment.
